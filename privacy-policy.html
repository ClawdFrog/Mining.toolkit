Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Overview

Mining Toolkit Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built this app to work entirely on your device without collecting any personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Mining Toolkit Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Your calculation inputs or results

How the App Works

Mining Toolkit Pro is an offline-first application. All content, calculations, and functionality are contained within the app itself. When you use our calculators or view drill specifications, all processing happens locally on your device.

Internet Connectivity

The app is designed to work without internet access. The only features that may use internet are:

  • Training video playback (if hosted externally)
  • App updates through the App Store

You can use all calculators, specifications, and reference materials completely offline.

Data Storage

Any preferences you set (theme, language, units) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Mining Toolkit Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13. It is a professional tool intended for use by trained mining personnel.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website.
